## Sprint Goal
1. Analyse behaviour improvement over multiple campaigns
2. Identify repeat offenders and high-risk patterns

## Step 1 — Behaviour KPIs (PM + Risk)

| KPI | Direction | Target |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Click Rate | ↓ Should decrease | < 15% by Week 6 |
| Report Rate | ↑ Should increase | > 15% by Week 6 |
| Training Completion | ↑ Should increase | > 80% by Week 6 |
| Repeat Offender Count | ↓ Should decrease | 0 by end of programme |

**Rules for Repeat Offender:**
- Clicked 2+ times across any campaigns, OR
- Clicked a phishing link AFTER completing the training module

## Step 3 — Repeat Offender Identification (Start-Up + Risk)

**Implementation:**  
`database.get_repeat_offenders()` queries events to find users who:
- clicked ≥ 2 times across all campaigns, **OR**
- clicked after a `training_completed` event (cross-joined subquery)

**Dashboard:**  
- New "🚨 Repeat Offenders" table on the Analytics page
- Shows: Name, Email, Department, Total Clicks, Campaigns Clicked, Risk Category, Clicked After Training
- **CSV Export** button: `GET /api/analytics/export-repeat-offenders`
